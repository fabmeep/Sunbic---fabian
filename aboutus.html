About Sunbic

Sunbic is an academical pioneer in the world of college. We aim to fortify the world with knowledge and innovation in hopes for a better future. Besides academical value we hold dear our attitude of humility, integrity and trust.

Our History

Sunbic Wong along with his friends decided to build a foundation in 2010 around the principles of solidarity, unity, nifty, bravery, integrity and consistency. These values are important because Sunbic Wong's successful life began since he started believing in those values. The first graduates of Sunbic have spread their believes and taught other people in various countries. While doing so SUNBIC was recognized as the pioneer of academical breakthrough because of how fast and effective the spread of knowledge and wisdom. The achievement sparked a great interest and began motivating the founders to build 2 other campuses around 2 years later.
