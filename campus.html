All of our campuses have the same facilities, which is renowned for its high quality

Lecture Rooms


Our lecture rooms are filled with Wegner Chairs and designed to optimize productivity during lectures. Hundreds of lecture rooms are spread acroos all three campus locations and each can be used for any major.

Basketball Courts


The floor is made with the same materials as NBA courts and the rings are adjustable. The courts can host a basketball tournament and hold 15,000 audience per court. Each campus has four basketball courts.

Library


The soothing sound of silence is always filling the libraries with sound-dampening walls designed to heighten the mood for learning or reading. The libraries are also filled with books from all over the globe, what you need it will provide.

Dining Hall


These grand dining halls are made to accompany students whether they are eating light or heavy meals. The spacious size isn't just for show, its function is to make the dining experience more relaxing so that anyone can enjoy their meals in the atmosphere.
